\# Momo IP Character



Momo is BunnyEra's official IP character and mascot.



\## Role



Momo represents:



\- BunnyEra brand identity

\- Friendly AI assistant

\- Cross-border ecommerce helper

\- Cute digital companion

\- Product onboarding guide



\## Visual Style



\- Cute bunny character

\- Soft 3D visual style

\- White body

\- Pink blush

\- Pink bow or decorative accent

\- Friendly expression

\- Clean background or transparent background
