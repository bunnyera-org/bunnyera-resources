## Public Use Notice

This document defines the long-term evolution roadmap for **momo**, BunnyEra's first symbolic digital citizen character.

This is a fictional character-development, brand, product-experience, and cross-media planning document. It is intended for roadmap planning, character evolution, UI companion development, storytelling, localization, accessibility, community content, and future media expansion.

momo should be presented as a fictional and symbolic BunnyEra character. Do not present momo as a real legal person, real citizen, sentient being, government authority, constitutional authority, legal advisor, medical advisor, therapist, emergency responder, or holder of real-world legal rights.

## 8. Risk Management

### 8.1 Brand Risk

**Risk:** momo is perceived as childish, confusing, or too decorative.  
**Mitigation:** Keep momo useful, clear, and product-aligned.

### 8.2 Legal/Personhood Confusion Risk

**Risk:** momo is interpreted as a real legal citizen or sentient entity.  
**Mitigation:** Use public-safe language and clear fictional disclaimers.

### 8.3 Cultural Risk

**Risk:** momo visual or story content misuses cultural symbols.  
**Mitigation:** Use local review, avoid sacred symbols, and follow the cultural protocol.

### 8.4 Privacy Risk

**Risk:** momo content accidentally exposes company or user information.  
**Mitigation:** Keep legal, tax, banking, and sensitive files out of public repositories.

### 8.5 Accessibility Risk

**Risk:** neon visuals or motion create usability issues.  
**Mitigation:** Provide high-contrast, reduced-motion, and text alternatives.

### 8.6 Overuse Risk

**Risk:** momo appears too often and distracts from serious tasks.  
**Mitigation:** Use momo where it improves clarity, comfort, or engagement.

---

## 9. Crisis and Correction Protocol

If momo content causes public confusion, cultural concern, privacy concern, or brand harm:

1. Pause distribution.
2. Identify the issue.
3. Acknowledge the concern.
4. Remove or revise the problematic content.
5. Avoid defensiveness.
6. Request local, legal, brand, or expert review if appropriate.
7. Update the relevant canon document.
8. Document the lesson in commit history or a changelog.

Example public correction:

```text
Thank you for the feedback. We are revising this momo content to make the character's fictional role clearer and to better respect the cultural context.
```
