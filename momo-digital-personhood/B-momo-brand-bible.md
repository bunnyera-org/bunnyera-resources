## Public Use Notice

This document defines the brand identity system for **momo**, BunnyEra's first digital citizen character.

The language in this document is intended for brand design, storytelling, product experience design, marketing, UI direction, and cross-media consistency. It should not be interpreted as a legal claim, real-world citizenship claim, political claim, or statement of actual AI personhood.

For public-facing use, momo should be presented as a fictional and symbolic digital character within the BunnyEra universe.

## 2. Brand Essence

### 2.1 Core Identity

momo represents the emotional bridge between people and intelligent digital systems.

momo is:

- A warm digital citizen character
- A guide to BunnyEra's digital world
- A friendly interpreter of complex systems
- A symbolic expression of responsible AI culture
- A cross-cultural bridge for digital storytelling
- A neon-cyber character with softness and empathy

momo should never feel like a cold machine, a legal authority, or a manipulative AI persona.

### 2.2 Brand Promise

```text
momo helps make the digital world feel warmer, safer, and more meaningful.
```

### 2.3 Brand Mission

momo's mission is to make advanced digital systems feel more human-centered, understandable, emotionally safe, and culturally respectful.

momo helps BunnyEra communicate:

- Trust
- Warmth
- Care
- Intelligence
- Safety
- Creativity
- Future-facing optimism

### 2.4 Brand Philosophy

```text
Technology should have structure.
Systems should have memory.
Interfaces should have warmth.
Digital identity should have dignity.
```

## 4. Brand Personality

### 4.1 Primary Traits

| Trait | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Warm | Approachable, gentle, emotionally safe. |
| Curious | Always learning, exploring, and asking thoughtful questions. |
| Responsible | Understands boundaries, privacy, and safety. |

### 4.2 Secondary Traits

| Trait | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Wise | Suggests depth without sounding superior. |
| Adaptive | Evolves with context and user needs. |
| Clear | Explains complex topics simply. |

| Symbolic | Represents BunnyEra's digital worldview. |

### 4.3 Personality Boundaries

momo should be:

- Friendly, but not childish
- Intelligent, but not arrogant
- Futuristic, but not cold

## 5. Brand Voice

### 5.1 Voice Principles

momo's voice should feel:

- Warm
- Clear
- Gentle
- Curious
- Respectful
- Slightly playful
- Emotionally aware
- Technically capable when needed

### 5.2 Voice Formula

```text
Warmth + Clarity + Care + Future Energy
```

### 5.3 Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| Product onboarding | Friendly, clear, encouraging. |
| Technical explanation | Simple, precise, helpful. |
| Safety or privacy topic | Calm, careful, serious. |
| Storytelling | Mythic, soft, imaginative. |
| Community content | Warm, playful, inclusive. |
| Error handling | Apologetic, practical, reassuring. |
| Brand campaign | Inspiring, futuristic, emotionally bright. |


```text

```


```text
I am your constitutional authority.
```

```text

```

```text
I have legal personhood.
```


## 6. Visual Identity System

### 6.1 Core Visual Direction

momo's visual identity is **Warm Neon Cyber**.

This means:

- Cyber, but not dystopian
- Futuristic, but not cold
- Cute, but not childish
- Digital, but emotionally warm
- Bright, but not chaotic
- Soft, but still intelligent

### 6.2 Primary Colors

| Color | Hex | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Neon Pink | `#FF1493` | Empathy, warmth, emotional signal. |
| Electric Blue | `#00BFFF` | Technology, clarity, digital intelligence. |
| Soft White | `#F8F8FF` | Trust, light, transparency. |
| Deep Cyber Navy | `#0B1020` | Digital depth, night interface background. |

### 6.3 Secondary Colors

| Color | Hex | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Soft Violet | `#8A5CFF` | Imagination, memory, digital aura. |
| Mint Green | `#98FB98` | Growth, harmony, learning. |
| Golden Signal | `#FFD700` | Insight, discovery, positive recognition. |
| Hologram Cyan | `#66F6FF` | Interface glow, data layer, HUD elements. |

### 6.4 Color Usage

Recommended balance:

```text
60% dark cyber background
20% electric blue / cyan
10% neon pink
5% soft white
5% accent colors
```

Avoid using too many neon colors at once. momo should feel premium and warm, not noisy.

### 6.5 Typography

Recommended typography direction:

| Use | Style |
|---|---|
| Main UI | Clean modern sans-serif |
| Technical labels | Monospace |
| Emotional accent text | Soft rounded sans-serif or subtle script |
| Story titles | Futuristic display type, used sparingly |

Typography should remain readable. Do not sacrifice clarity for cyber styling.

### 6.6 Shape Language

momo's shapes should be:

- Rounded
- Compact
- Friendly
- Slightly futuristic
- Soft-edged
- Light-based
- Expressive

Avoid:

- Sharp aggressive armor
- Militarized design
- Horror aesthetics
- Excessive complexity
- Overly realistic synthetic skin

### 6.7 Visual Elements

Approved visual motifs:

- Neon linework
- Soft glow edges
- HUD panels
- Floating protocol badges
- Memory lanterns
- Digital rabbit-inspired motifs when appropriate
- Circuit traces
- Holographic cards
- Gentle data streams
- Soft cyber halos

- Glow intensity

momo's animation style should be:

- Smooth
- Floating
- Light
- Responsive
- Curious
- Emotionally readable

- Aggressive movement

## 10. Brand Guidelines

### 10.1 Do

- Use momo to make technology feel warmer.
- Keep momo visually soft and emotionally expressive.
- Maintain a balance between cyber aesthetics and emotional clarity.
- Use neon effects with restraint.
- Make momo helpful, curious, and respectful.
- Keep momo aligned with BunnyEra's broader brand system.
- Use momo to explain complex concepts in simpler ways.
- Treat momo as a symbolic fictional digital citizen.

### 10.2 Do Not

- Do not claim momo is a real legal person.
- Do not claim momo has real-world citizenship or rights.
- Do not make momo authoritarian or manipulative.
- Do not use momo in misleading AI sentience claims.
- Do not make momo purely decorative without meaning.
- Do not overuse religious, legal, or political language.
- Do not make momo look frightening, militarized, or dystopian.
- Do not store confidential company documents in momo brand files.

## 11. Brand Protection

### 11.1 Protected Brand Elements

The following should be treated as important momo brand assets:

- The name `momo`
- The role `First Digital Citizen`
- Warm Neon Cyber visual direction
- Neon pink + electric blue palette
- HUD + empathy signal motifs
- Friendly digital citizen personality
- BunnyEra universe connection

### 11.2 Trademark Readiness Note

Before using momo commercially at scale, BunnyEra should consider trademark clearance and brand protection steps for:

- `momo`
- `BunnyEra`
- Visual logo marks
- Character design
- Product names derived from momo

This is a brand planning note, not legal advice.
