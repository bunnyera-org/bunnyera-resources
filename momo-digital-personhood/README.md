## Overview

This directory contains the public-safe canon documents for **momo**, BunnyEra's first symbolic digital citizen character.

momo is a fictional and symbolic BunnyEra character designed to represent warmth, clarity, privacy awareness, cross-cultural respect, and future-facing digital storytelling.

The term **Digital Citizen** is used as an in-universe brand and storytelling concept. It does **not** mean real-world citizenship, legal personhood, government recognition, professional authority, or actual AI consciousness.

---

## Public Use Notice

momo should be presented as a **fictional and symbolic digital character** within the BunnyEra universe.

Do not present momo as:

- A real legal person
- A real citizen
- A sentient being as a factual claim
- A government authority
- A constitutional authority
- A legal, medical, tax, financial, or mental-health professional
- An emergency responder
- A holder of real-world legal rights

Use this system for brand, story, character, visual, product, and cross-media consistency.
