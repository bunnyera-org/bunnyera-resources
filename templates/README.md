\# BunnyEra Templates



This folder stores public templates used across BunnyEra projects.



\## Template Types



Examples:



\- README templates

\- Product description templates

\- Launch note templates

\- Documentation templates

\- Social media templates

\- Internal workflow templates

\- Brand copy templates



\## Usage



Templates can be reused in:



\- BunnyEra Docs

\- BunnyEra Console

\- BunnyEra AI prompts

\- BunnyEra marketing materials

\- BunnyEra product pages



\## Rules



\- Use clear filenames.

\- Keep templates simple and reusable.

\- Do not include private customer information.

\- Do not include API keys or secrets.

\- Keep templates public-safe.
